Frequently asked questions

If a venue declines your request, it moves from the Pending tab to the Declined tab in your bookings. There's no penalty to your account — you're free to request other venues right away. On the declined booking you'll see a Join waitlist option: if the venue's date later becomes available (due to a cancellation or change of plans), you'll be notified and can submit a new request. Declined requests can be archived once you're done with them.

Cancellations can happen on either side, or automatically if payment isn't completed within 48 hours of approval. Either way, the booking moves to the Cancelled tab in your bookings, and the date is released back to the venue's calendar. You can use the Rebook a date → button on any cancelled booking to return to that venue's listing and request a different date. Refund timing follows the venue's cancellation policy and GigNVenue's cancellation terms, both of which you acknowledged at the time of booking.

Search for venues on the main page, open the venue profile for any listing that looks right, and click "Request to book." Fill in your event details — type, date, expected attendance, and any notes — verify that you've reviewed the venue's terms, and submit. The host reviews your request and you'll receive a notification once they respond, usually within 1–2 business days. Although you'll see the exact numbers due upon booking approval, no payment is required at this stage.

Yes. You can send requests to multiple venues for the same date simultaneously — there's no penalty for doing so while you're still deciding. If a venue approves your request and you complete payment, any other pending requests for that same date are automatically cancelled and noted in your booking history — so you can revisit those venues for next time.

Hosts are strongly encouraged to respond within 1–2 business days. If a host hasn't responded in that time, you're completely free, as always, to request other venues — and you can cancel a request at any time with no penalty to your account and no obligation to wait indefinitely.

You'll receive a notification that your booking has been approved. You then have 48 hours to complete payment — a 20% venue deposit plus an 8% booking fee, the same figures you saw before submitting your request. Once payment is received, your date is locked and confirmed. If payment isn't completed within 48 hours, the approval expires and the date becomes available again on the venue's calendar.

The deposit is 20% of the venue's nightly rate. It's collected by GigNVenue when you confirm your booking and held securely until after your event. Within 24 hours of the event playing off, the full deposit is released to the venue. The deposit is not GigNVenue's income — it acts as a security hold that protects both you and the venue.

The booking fee is 8% of the venue's nightly rate and is charged to you when a booking is confirmed. This below-market, non-refundable fee covers payment processing, platform infrastructure, and customer support. Venue owners are never charged any fee. Browsing venues and submitting requests is always free.

Yes, just click the heart icon wherever you see it and it will be stored in your Saved Venues. Access them anytime from the Saved Venues section in your dashboard. It's a great way to build a shortlist before you're ready to commit to a date.

Saving a venue also keeps you in the loop — when a venue you've saved posts a Featured Night (a proactively promoted date, sometimes at a special rate), you'll see an alert on your dashboard Overview so you can browse and request before the date is taken.

Yes — the Tour Planner in your dashboard lets you add multiple stops (city + date) for an upcoming tour. For each stop, GigNVenue shows you available venues in that area. Select the ones you want and submit booking requests to all of them in one go.

Our support team is available via the contact page. You can also browse the full Help Center for more detailed answers.
