Our story

Built by music lovers, for the people who keep live music alive

GigNVenue exists because finding and booking the right venue has always been harder than it needs to be — and because the artists and venues that make live music happen deserve better tools.

Our mission

Make the path from artist to stage as clear as possible

Live music is one of the most powerful things humans do together. It creates community, launches careers, preserves culture, and fills rooms with a kind of energy that nothing else quite replicates. But behind every great show is a booking process that is still, in most cases, held together with cold emails, unanswered DMs, and personal connections that not every artist has access to.

GigNVenue was built to change that. We believe that the quality of your music — not who you know or how persistent your emails are — should be the deciding factor in whether you get on a stage. And we believe that great venue owners deserve a way to fill their calendar with great acts, without relying on word-of-mouth alone.

So we built a marketplace. Simple to use, transparent about fees, and designed specifically for live music — not repurposed from an events platform that was built for weddings and corporate functions.

What we stand for

The values we build everything around

Transparency first

Every fee is visible before you commit to anything. There are no hidden charges, no surprise deductions, and no fine print that changes how much anyone earns or pays. What you see is what you get.

Music-specific by design

We're not a generic event platform with a music category bolted on. Every feature — the amenity filters, the hold priority system, the deposit structure, the messaging flow — was built with live music in mind.

Fair to both sides

Artists and venue owners are equally important to this platform. We don't take sides — we build tools that protect both, create accountability on both, and make the booking experience better for everyone involved.

Access over exclusivity

The best artists aren't always the most connected ones. GigNVenue gives independent and emerging artists the same access to great venues as established acts — because the music should speak for itself.

Simple enough to actually use

Great tools shouldn't require a manual. We obsess over making every interaction on GigNVenue clear, fast, and low-friction — so you spend less time managing bookings and more time making music.

The stage is where artists earn

Artists pay a 5% booking fee when they book. For venues, it's just 5% — a quarter of the deposit — earned only after a show successfully plays off, while their 80% nightly rate is negotiated and collected entirely on their own terms. Both sides contribute a small, fair share to keep this platform running. A booked night is a ticket link, a merch table, a room full of new fans — our fee exists so we can keep making that happen.

We grow when you grow

Our only revenue is a 10% total booking fee — 5% from artists at confirmation, 5% from venues at deposit release — earned only when a show successfully plays off. No subscriptions, no advertising, no dark night surcharges. Your success is our business model.

Where we're headed

This is just the beginning

GigNVenue launched with a focus on the USA — building a catalog of quality venues across every market, from dive bars in Austin to amphitheaters in Colorado. But the vision is bigger than that.

🇺🇸

USA launch

Starting with the country that has one of the richest live music traditions in the world. Covering every city, every genre, every venue size — from 50-cap listening rooms to 10,000-cap amphitheaters.

💳

Secure Stripe payments

Deposits and booking fees are collected and held securely via Stripe — a globally trusted payment processor. Deposits are released to venues automatically after a show plays off. We built the infrastructure. Stripe handles the money.

🌍

International expansion

The world has great music venues and great artists in every country. Once the US market is thriving, GigNVenue will expand market by market — with full multi-currency support from day one.

📱

Mobile apps

A dedicated app experience for artists on the move and venue hosts managing their calendar from the stage door. Built with the same clarity and simplicity as the web platform.

🎙️

Artist profiles & discovery

A richer profile system where artists can showcase their music, draw size, and past performances — helping venue hosts make better, more informed booking decisions.

🤝

Industry partnerships

Connecting GigNVenue with agents, promoters, and management companies — so the platform becomes a natural part of how the live music industry actually operates.
