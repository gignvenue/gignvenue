Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective: March 2026

GigNVenue ("GigNVenue," "we," "us," or "our") operates the GigNVenue platform, a live music venue booking marketplace connecting artists and bookers with venue owners across the United States. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By creating an account or using the GigNVenue platform, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you create an account or use the platform, we collect information you provide, which may include:

  • Account information: first and last name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile information: artist or band name, profile photo, bio, genre, social media links, and other details you add to your public profile
  • Venue information (hosts): venue name, address, capacity, photos, amenities, nightly rate, and cancellation policy
  • Booking information: event type, expected attendance, event notes, requested dates, and related booking communications
  • Messages: content of messages exchanged between artists and venue hosts through the GigNVenue messaging system, including pre-booking messages initiated before a formal booking request is submitted and booking messages associated with an active booking
  • Report and flag submissions: when you submit a report or flag about another user, venue listing, or message, we collect the details of that report, including the reported content, your description of the concern, any supporting context you provide, and the date of submission
  • Payment information: billing details submitted at checkout. Card data is processed directly by Stripe and is never stored on GigNVenue servers

Information collected automatically

When you use the platform, we may automatically collect:

  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Referring URLs and session data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate booking requests, confirmations, cancellations, and payments between artists and venue hosts
  • Release contact details between parties at the appropriate point in the booking process (see Section 4)
  • Send transactional communications: booking notifications, payment confirmations, reminders, and account alerts
  • Process payments and manage deposits held on behalf of users via Stripe
  • Resolve disputes between artists and venue hosts
  • Maintain the safety, security, and integrity of the platform
  • Improve platform features and user experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your information to serve third-party advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

With other users

Certain information is visible to other users of the platform as part of normal platform operation:

  • Artist profile information (name, band name, photo, bio, genre) is visible to venue hosts from the moment a booking request is submitted
  • Venue listing information is publicly visible to all users
  • Contact details (email and phone number) are shared between an artist and a venue host only after payment has been completed for a confirmed booking (see Section 4)

With service providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform:

  • Stripe: payment processing and deposit management. Stripe processes payment information directly and maintains its own privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers: cloud services used to store and serve platform data
  • Analytics providers: aggregated, non-personally identifiable usage data to improve the platform

We do not authorize service providers to use your personal information for their own marketing purposes.

For legal reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of GigNVenue, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of GigNVenue's assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users of any such change in ownership or control of personal information.

4. Contact Detail Release Policy

GigNVenue operates a deliberate, staged approach to contact detail sharing that protects both artists and venue hosts throughout the booking process.

  • From the moment a booking request is received, venue hosts can see an artist's full name, profile photo, and band name, and can access the artist's full profile
  • An artist's personal contact details — email address and phone number — are shared with the venue host only after payment for a confirmed booking has been successfully completed via Stripe
  • Similarly, a venue host's direct contact details are shared with the artist only after payment has been completed

This policy exists to protect personal information until both parties have made a financial commitment to the booking, and to ensure that the GigNVenue platform remains the secure channel for all pre-payment communications.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by GigNVenue and our infrastructure partners. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Payment card data is never stored on GigNVenue servers. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant.

No method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the platform services. Specifically:

  • Account data: retained for the life of your account. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7)
  • Booking history: retained for a minimum of three years after a booking's event date, to support dispute resolution and financial record-keeping
  • Booking messages: retained for the life of the associated booking or account, whichever is longer
  • Pre-booking messages: retained for a minimum of 12 months after the last message in the thread, or for the life of the associated artist or venue account, whichever is longer. Pre-booking message threads that lead to a confirmed booking are retained for the same period as booking messages
  • Report data: records of submitted reports, including their content and any action taken in response, are retained for a minimum of three years to support ongoing moderation, dispute investigation, and compliance obligations
  • Payment records: retained as required by applicable financial regulations, typically seven years

Upon account deletion, we will remove or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business necessity.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information. Users in California have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • Right to know: the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to delete: request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to opt out of sale: GigNVenue does not sell personal information. This right is not applicable, but we state it plainly: we will never sell your data
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our support page or email us directly. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

GigNVenue uses browser local storage to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and support dashboard functionality (such as pending booking requests and notification state). We may also use standard cookies for session management and analytics.

You can clear local storage and cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect your ability to use certain platform features, including staying logged in.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies.

9. Children's Privacy

GigNVenue is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify users via email or a prominent notice on the platform prior to the change taking effect, and we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of GigNVenue after any changes to this Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us:

  • Support page: gignvenue.com/contact
  • Email: privacy@gignvenue.com
